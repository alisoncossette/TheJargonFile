gas

1. interj. A term of disgust and hatred, implying that gas should be
dispensed in generous quantities, thereby exterminating the source of
irritation. Some loser just reloaded the system for no reason! Gas! 2.
interj. A suggestion that someone or something ought to be flushed out of
mercy. The system's getting wedged every few minutes. Gas! 3. vt. To flush
(sense 1). You should gas that old crufty software. 4.
